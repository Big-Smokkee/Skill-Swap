# Skill Swap 🔁
## Purpose
Skill Swap is a community-driven skill exchange platform designed to connect people who want to learn new skills with individuals who are willing to share their expertise. The platform promotes collaborative learning by allowing users to exchange knowledge instead of relying solely on traditional courses or paid training programs.

For example, a user who is highly skilled in public speaking but wants to learn guitar playing or MERN stack development can connect with another user who possesses those skills. In return, the second user can improve their communication and presentation abilities through mentorship from the public speaking expert. This creates a mutually beneficial learning environment where both participants gain valuable knowledge and practical experience.

The platform also allows users to discover mentors, explore available skills, and book learning sessions for skills they have always wanted to learn.
